Clinical utility

Somatic regulation at the point of crisis.

Surge is a 90-second somatic circuit breaker designed for acute nervous system dysregulation. It delivers a deterministic decay protocol — visual, auditory, and haptic — without gamification, sentiment analysis, or persistent patient accounts. Providers deploy it as a discrete intervention between sessions or at escalation points.

Licensing model

Surge operates on an annual clinical license. Your organization receives a provisioned token pool for anonymous patient access. Tokens validate at runtime without storing patient identity — no accounts, no PHI in the Surge layer. Usage telemetry is session-scoped and de-identified. Heron, the post-regulation guide, activates only after a completed or partial cycle when clinically indicated.
